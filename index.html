About Us

Over 20 crore Indians still go to sleep on an empty stomach everyday, in a world where we are progressing in every way. Hunger no more is community of students who want to serve the humanity and help them as much as possible. If we could make even a small difference in the lives of 1000 people, our goal would be achieved. Our mission is to Reduce the wastage of food by using it for a good cause and provide a healthy meal to kids so that they can sleep with a full stomach. Our future plan is to collaborate with different NGOs, old age homes so more benefit of the society. Also, we will combine with mess of all over Kota city to reduce the wastage and use it for a good cause as much as possible.
